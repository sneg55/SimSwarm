BITCOIN HALVING TRIGGERS MARKET FRENZY AND MINING CRISIS

New York, March 2026 — Bitcoin's fifth block reward halving occurred at block 1,050,000, cutting miner rewards from 3.125 BTC to 1.5625 BTC. The price stood at $78,400 at the moment of halving, triggering an immediate wave of activity across markets.

MARKET REACTION:

MicroStrategy chairman Michael Saylor posted on X: "The halving is the most predictable supply shock in financial history. We are adding to our position." MicroStrategy holds 380,000 BTC worth $29.8 billion.

BlackRock's iShares Bitcoin Trust (IBIT) saw $1.2 billion in inflows in the 48 hours following the halving, according to Bloomberg data. BlackRock CEO Larry Fink called Bitcoin "a legitimate asset class that deserves a place in diversified portfolios."

Cathie Wood's ARK Invest raised its 2030 Bitcoin price target from $1 million to $1.5 million, citing the halving's supply dynamics. ARK analyst Yassine Elmandjra published a 40-page report arguing this cycle would see "institutional adoption at unprecedented scale."

JPMorgan analyst Nikolaos Panigirtzoglou published a note warning that "the halving is already priced in" and predicting Bitcoin would trade between $60,000 and $90,000 for the next six months, citing diminishing returns from each successive halving cycle.

MINING INDUSTRY:

Marathon Digital CEO Fred Thiel announced the company would shut down operations at three facilities in Texas and North Dakota where electricity costs exceed $0.06/kWh post-halving. "Approximately 15-20% of the global hash rate will go offline within 30 days," Thiel told CNBC.

Riot Platforms CEO Jason Les confirmed a $150 million investment in next-generation Bitmain S21 Pro miners, stating the company can remain profitable above $45,000 per BTC.

CleanSpark CEO Zach Bradford announced the acquisition of two distressed mining facilities in Georgia from a smaller operator forced to close post-halving, paying $35 million.

Peter Thiel's Founders Fund increased its Bitcoin allocation by $200 million, while George Soros's family office disclosed a new position in Bitcoin futures.

COMMUNITY SENTIMENT:

Crypto influencer Anthony Pompliano tweeted: "Every halving, they say it's priced in. Every halving, they're wrong." The post received 45 million views.

On Reddit's r/Bitcoin, the top post titled "HODL through the halving — this time IS different" received 25,000 upvotes. Meanwhile, r/Buttcoin's top post "Imagine thinking cutting supply of a speculative asset matters" sparked a 3,000-comment debate.

Ethereum co-founder Vitalik Buterin commented: "Bitcoin's monetary policy is elegant but its energy consumption remains concerning. Proof-of-stake solved this years ago."

Binance CEO Richard Teng reported a 340% increase in spot trading volume in the 24 hours surrounding the halving event.

Let's seed the ecosystem
Upload a document or paste text to begin. The swarm will extract entities and build a knowledge graph.
What makes a good seed? ▶
✓ Specific events — an earnings report, a policy announcement, a product launch press release.
✓ Multiple stakeholders — documents mentioning companies, regulators, media, and public figures.
✓ Recent context — news articles or analyst notes from the last few days.
○ 2,000–20,000 characters is the sweet spot. Too short = no context, too long = noise.
✗ Avoid generic content — boilerplate pages or Wikipedia summaries won't produce interesting results.
